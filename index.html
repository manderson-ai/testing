Information Technology & Digital Transformation

Unlock your potential in the digital world

Essential Question

How can understanding information technology and digital transformation empower you to become both a responsible digital citizen and an innovative creator in an increasingly connected world?

Start Learning

Learning Objective

Students will be able to identify and explain key concepts in information technology and digital transformation, including cybersecurity principles, computing systems, and career pathways, while developing technical and critical thinking skills through hands-on activities and reflective practices.

Success Criteria

01

Digital Glossary Creation

Students can demonstrate understanding of fundamental IT concepts by creating a comprehensive digital glossary that includes accurate definitions of key terms such as artificial intelligence, programming, networking, cybersecurity, web design, and development.

02

Cybersecurity Application

Students can apply cybersecurity principles to protect personal information by developing strong passwords, identifying potential security threats, and implementing appropriate security measures in real-world scenarios.

Reflection Prompts

Digital Identity

Reflect on your digital footprint across various platforms. How do your online activities shape your digital identity? What steps can you take to ensure your digital presence positively represents who you are while maintaining security?

AI Support

I notice you're thinking about your digital identity. Consider how potential employers or schools might view your social media. What specific changes could you make to better align your digital presence with your future goals? Have you considered creating content that showcases your IT skills and interests?

Technology Impact

Consider a technology you use daily. How has this technology changed over time, and how might it evolve in the next five years? What potential positive and negative impacts could these changes have on society?

AI Support

Your analysis of technology evolution is interesting. To deepen your thinking, consider: What specific groups might benefit most from these changes? Who might be disadvantaged? What ethical considerations should developers keep in mind? How might you personally contribute to ensuring this technology develops in a positive direction?

Career Exploration

Based on what you've learned about IT and digital transformation careers, which pathway interests you most and why? What skills do you already possess that align with this pathway, and what skills would you need to develop?

AI Support

I see you're interested in exploring IT career paths. Have you considered exploring projects or certifications in your area of interest? What specific roles match your strengths? Consider researching professionals in this field on LinkedIn or industry forums - what education and experience paths did they take? What specific next steps could you take while still in school to move toward this career?
